Vehicle Pricing Analysis

Pricing Insights

  • Convertibles command the highest average price, followed by Trucks
  • Model year has the strongest correlation with price (newer = higher)
  • Vehicle condition significantly impacts pricing, with "excellent" commanding premium
  • Sedans represent the largest volume but middle-tier pricing

Sales Timing Analysis

Timing Insights

  • Vehicles under $20K show sharp sales increase after 30 days
  • Premium vehicles ($20K+) sell more consistently over time
  • Paint color affects sales speed - neutral colors sell faster
  • New condition vehicles sell significantly faster than used

Strategic Recommendations

Data Processing Methodology

Cleaned and processed 51,525 vehicle records, handling missing values and standardizing categories. Removed outliers and grouped similar vehicle types for meaningful analysis.

Key Business Insights

  • Implement dynamic pricing for vehicles under $20K after 25 days to accelerate sales
  • Focus inventory on trucks and convertibles for higher profit margins
  • Price adjustments after 30 days can reduce carrying costs significantly
  • Vehicle condition is the strongest predictor of both price and sale speed
  • Seasonal pricing strategies can optimize inventory turnover
