Vehicle Pricing Analysis

Pricing Insights

  • Buses and trucks command the highest average prices ($17K+)
  • Convertibles and coupes are premium vehicles ($14K+ range)
  • Vehicle condition is the strongest price predictor - new vehicles ($25K) vs fair condition ($3K)
  • Model year significantly impacts pricing - newer vehicles command premium prices
  • Clear price hierarchy: Luxury > Utility > Standard passenger vehicles

Strategic Recommendations

Key Business Insights

  • Vehicles under $20K show sharp sales increase after 30 days, indicating price sensitivity
  • Dynamic pricing strategy: Moderate pricing sooner to sell vehicles faster at higher prices
  • Early price adjustments (25-30 days) reduce carrying costs while maximizing profits
  • Focus inventory on high-margin vehicles: trucks, convertibles, and newer model years
  • Vehicle condition and model year are the strongest predictors of both price and sales speed
